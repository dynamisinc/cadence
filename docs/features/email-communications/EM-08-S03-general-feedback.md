# Story: EM-08-S03 - General Feedback

**As a** User,  
**I want** to send general feedback or questions,  
**So that** I can communicate with the Cadence team.

## Context

A general contact form for questions, comments, or feedback that doesn't fit bug reports or feature requests.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] **Given** I access Contact/Feedback, **when** form opens, **then** I see category selector (Question, Feedback, Other)
- [ ] **Given** form, **when** filling out, **then** I provide: category, subject, message
- [ ] **Given** form submitted, **when** saved, **then** email sent to support address
- [ ] **Given** submission, **when** successful, **then** I see confirmation

## Out of Scope

- Live chat
- FAQ/knowledge base

## Dependencies

- EM-01-S01: ACS Email Configuration

## Effort Estimate

**2 story points** - Simple contact form
